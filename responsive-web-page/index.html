He's smart. He's lovable. He's Dexter Morgan, America's favorite serial killer, who spends his days solving crimes and his nights committing them.

Who is Dexter?

He’s this caring dad, devoted husband (well, kind of...), and a helpful coworker at Miami Metro. But then, you know... he’s out there wrapping people in plastic and "taking out the trash" at night.

What's in Dexter's bag?
  • A knife
  • A Drug
  • A trophy
  • Annd.. his last victim in pieces
Dexter's Code?
  • Thou shalt not kill an innocent person.
  • Thou shalt not get caught
  • Thou shalt kill only those who deserve to die.
  • Thou shalt have no emotional attachment to the victim.
